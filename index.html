In Bosnia & Herzegovina, coffee is meant for socializing. Another way coffee time is reffered to there, is the term “ćejf” (cheyf) which can be translated as pleasure or the act of enjoying something.

  • * DJEZVA: copper coffee pot, used for heating up the coffee on a stove.

  • + FIlDJAN: ceramic espresso sized cup for drinking kafa, and kafa only.

  • # SECER: holds sugar cubes for dipping into the coffee.

  • ~ TABLA: copper tray that holds coffee set, usually hand crafted design.
